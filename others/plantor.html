Plantor Native App

Problem:
  • Users couldn’t grow plants successfully because they didn't know what they can grow at their place
  • Whether the plants are getting sufficient lighting
  • Whether the temperature is suitable for the plants
  • Whether the outdoor plants are grown during the right season and weather conditions

Solution:
  • Creating a mobile app to solve the problem
  • Provide plant suggestions based on location, season, and light exposure
  • Allow users to save their location’s environment information in the app to view suitable plants of the specific environment in the future
  • Provide notifications about seasonal changes and weather hazards

My Roles & Responsibilities:
  • Delegate tasks to development team
  • Front-end Development - creating navigation, plants suggestions flow, Hi-Fi styling
  • Back-end Development - running backend end points on EC2 instance, adding ssl certification for https, securing the endpoints with auth validation
  • Version control - using github to control and manage code integration, creating issues and bug tracking
